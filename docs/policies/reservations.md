# Lab Reservations

Reserve time in the XR Immersive Media Lab using our online booking system.

<a href="https://www.supersaas.com/schedule/IML/ImmersiveMediaLab" target="_blank" class="md-button md-button--primary">Book a Reservation</a>

## How to Make a Reservation

1. Click the booking link above or visit [SuperSaaS Scheduling](https://www.supersaas.com/schedule/IML/ImmersiveMediaLab)
2. Select the date and time slot you need
3. Fill in your details (name, email, purpose of visit)
4. Submit your reservation
5. You'll receive a confirmation email

## Reservation Policies

- **Book in advance:** Reservations should be made at least 24 hours ahead
- **Cancellations:** Cancel at least 12 hours before your scheduled time
- **No-shows:** Multiple no-shows may result in booking restrictions
- **Maximum time:** [Add your max booking duration, e.g., "2 hours per session"]
- **Who can book:** CAMD students, faculty, and staff with valid Northeastern credentials
