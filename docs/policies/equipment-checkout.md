# Equipment Checkout Guide

Rent equipment from the Media Center using the WebCheckout Patron Portal.

<a href="https://webcheckout.northeastern.edu" target="_blank" class="md-button md-button--primary">Open WebCheckout</a>

---

## Pickup & Return Policy

!!! warning "Important Rules"
    - **Maximum rental:** 3 business days — no exceptions, no extensions
    - **Arrive on time:** No earlier or later than 30 minutes from your pickup time
    - **No-shows:** Reservations cancelled after 30 minutes

### Hours (Fall & Spring Semesters)

| | Monday–Thursday | Friday |
|---|---|---|
| **Earliest pickup/return** | 9:30 AM | 9:30 AM |
| **Latest pickup/return** | 5:00 PM | 4:00 PM |
| **Same-day cutoff** | 4:00 PM | 3:00 PM |

!!! tip "Weekend Rentals"
    The XR Immersive Media Lab is closed on weekends. Pick up Wednesday–Friday and return the following week.

---

## How to Create a Reservation

### Step 1: Start a New Reservation

1. Click **"New Reservation"** or the **New** button
2. Select **"Immersive Media Lab"** as your Checkout Center
3. Dismiss the "Message of the Day" after reading

### Step 2: Set Your Pickup & Return Time

1. Select your **pickup date and time**
2. Select your **return date and time**
3. Click the **✓** icon to save after each change

!!! danger "Pay Attention!"
    Choosing the wrong date or time could prevent pickup or result in late fees. There's a 1-hour lead time for same-day pickups.

### Step 3: Browse the Catalog

1. Scroll down and click **"Browse Catalog"**
2. Select a category (Headsets, 360 Cameras, etc.)
3. Navigate subcategories until you find your equipment
4. Click the **+** icon to add items to your cart

!!! info "Item Limits"
    Some items (360 Cameras and Headsets) are limited to **1 per person**. You cannot have overlapping reservations with the same item type. For special requests, please contact the XRIML Lab manager at: s.ouk@northeastern.edu

### Step 4: Review & Submit

1. Click **"Return to Reservation"** to view your cart
2. Read and accept the **terms and conditions**
3. Click **"Submit Reservation"**
4. You'll receive an email confirmation

## Picking Up Equipment?

The person picking up must bring their Husky ID. 
**We do not release equipment to anyone who did not make the WebCheckout Reservation themselves!**

---

## Editing or Cancelling

You can edit or cancel your reservation **up to 1 hour before pickup**.

1. From the home screen, click your reservation (identified by CK number)
2. Click **"Edit"** or **"Cancel"** from the Actions menu

After the 1-hour window, contact the XRIML directly.

---

## Common Errors & Solutions

| Error | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Incorrect date/time | Adjust pickup and return times within policy guidelines |
| Resource exceeds limit | Remove duplicate items or adjust dates so reservations don't overlap |
| Minimum checkout period | Ensure at least 4 hours between pickup and return |
| Resource unavailable | Click the **⚠️** icon → **Replace** to find an alternative |
